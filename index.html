SaleShow — Sales & Inventory Intelligence Platform

Visit Site Production

A comprehensive B2B analytics portal designed for suppliers and buyers to monitor sellout performance, inventory levels, and purchase orders across multiple retail branches. Built with Laravel and React, SaleShow consolidates complex data ingestion workflows, visual analytics, and operational intelligence into a single, role-based platform with advanced access controls and real-time processing capabilities.

Key Features & Achievements
  • Data Ingestion at Scale: Excel imports processing multiple sheets (sellout, recepciones, existencia, clasificación) via Laravel Excel with queue-based chunked reading and server-side validation.
  • Analytics Dashboards: Interactive Recharts visualizations displaying ordered vs. received metrics, top-moving products, and low stock alerts with DateRangePicker and quick preset filters.
  • Purchase Order Management: Complete PO lifecycle tracking with received/partial/pending status KPIs and expandable product rows for detailed drill-down analysis.
  • Inventory Intelligence: Branch-level restock urgency algorithms analyzing stock levels, monthly sales velocity, and product classification with dynamic color coding and automated weekly email alerts.
  • Supplier Portal: Comprehensive provider management with status tracking, bulk account creation capabilities, and backend-delegated logo routing with Stripe-gated access control.
  • Role-Based Access Control: Granular permission system separating suppliers, buyers, and administrators with activity isolation by branch and supplier relationships.
  • DevOps & Infrastructure: DigitalOcean App Platform deployment with staging/production pipelines, DO Spaces object storage, and Redis-backed queue processing.
